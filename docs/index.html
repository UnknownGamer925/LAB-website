We are Lympstone Academy Brass

We are the academy band for the SW Comms Brass Band
(a championship section band sponsored by South West
Communications) that performs at a range of local public
and charity events. We are not a contesting band and only
available for event hire.

Our aim as an academy band is to encourage people, especially
the younger generations, to enjoy the world of brass banding
and, if they want to, ultimately join the main band. We have a
mix of abilities, ages and experience from new starters to
professional players with years of brass band experience and
we are open to all to come and join us.

Join Us!

To Listen

We practise every Saturday from 10am to 12pm in the practice room on Chapel Road. The door is always open for anyone to come in and listen to us with free tea, coffee and squash during our break (around 11am). We also preform at many events so keep an eye out for one near you!

To Play

We are a beginner friendly band. Anyone can join as long as they can read music and make a sound on their instrument of choice! We can even lend you an instrument if you don't own one (subject to availability). For more details see the 'Become a member' page.

Hire Us!

We're available for hire in the local area so if you're organising an event and want us to perform at it, give us a call! Rates are decided on a per-event basis dependent on location, length of performance and more.
